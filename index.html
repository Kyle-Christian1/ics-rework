About Us

Since its inception, Instrument Control Services has been at the forefront of providing automation design, electrical / instrumentation construction and maintenance, facilities operations support, and valve repair services to a broad spectrum of industries. Clients include the aerospace, refining, manufacturing, oil field, semi-conductor, agriculture, water treatment and bio-tech industries located throughout the Southwestern United States. Our clients look to us for the design and implementation of control systems as well as full time maintenance and operations crews to support their facility. Bottom line: ICS can meet all your needs.

Contract Services

ICS PARTNER IN OPERATIONS

ICS has been providing qualified crews to work at our clients' facilities for the support of operations for many years. We can provide a full range of craft, operations, labor or technical personnel in both large and small numbers. These crews will be led by ICS supervisory personnel who can direct the day to day tasks requested by the client. The employees on the crew remain ICS employees throughout their assignment and all statutory taxes, insurance and benefits are provided by ICS. Clients pay hourly billing rate for all hours worked and at the end of the assignment, workers are reassigned to other ICS clients or are released — thereby the customer is relieved of termination paperwork and administration. ICS will work with the client's Human Resources and Environmental Safety and Health departments to make sure our policies and procedures do not conflict with the client's policies and procedures. Daily work, incident reports and safety meeting are all handled by our administration staff. This allows the client to focus on their core business not our employees.

PERSONEL RESOURCES INCLUDE

California Certified electricians with expertise in all phases of electrical work • Instrument technicians with calibration, troubleshooting and repair experience • Experienced operators of process systems to low cost, entry-level roustabouts • Pneumatic instrumentation and tubing installers • Valve technician crews for scheduled testing and maintenance • Professional supervisors for management of projects and personnel • Systems integrators for PLC and SCADA programming and implementation • Technical Writers for documentation of electrical schematics and P&ID's

SAFETY & QUALITY CONTROL

ICS places the utmost importance on the well-being and safety of our employees and clients. Our policy is to provide a program of ongoing training and technology that will afford our clients and ourselves the safest possible working environment. Working safely enhances our ability to provide our clients with the highest quality products and services while minimizing costs and liabilities.
